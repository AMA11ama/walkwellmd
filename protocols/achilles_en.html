WalkWellMD

Achilles Tendon Rupture – Rehabilitation Program

By WalkWellMD

Week 0–2

  • Plantarflexion cast
  • Non–weight bearing with crutches

Week 2–4

  • Walker boot with 2–4 cm heel lift
  • Protected weight-bearing in boot with crutches:
    • Week 2–3: 25%
    • Week 3–4: 50%
    • Week 4–5: 75%
    • Week 5–6: 100%

Week 4–6

  • Active ankle ROM:
    • Plantarflexion & dorsiflexion to neutral only
    • Inversion & eversion below neutral
  • Do not go past neutral during exercises
